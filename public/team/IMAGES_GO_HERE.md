| File           | Person              | Format        |
|----------------|---------------------|---------------|
| `manraj.jpg`   | Manraj Marhala      | JPG or WebP   |
| `rajiv.jpg`    | Rajiv Singh         | JPG or WebP   |

## Recommended specs
- **Aspect ratio:** 3:4 (portrait orientation)
- **Minimum size:** 600 × 800 px
- **Focus:** Face/chest up, centered
- **Background:** Neutral / blurred (studio or outdoor blur)
